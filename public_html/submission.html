ABSTRACT GUIDELINE

The abstract should be uploaded in easy chair platform (under Registration and Submission button below).

Be concise, be clear, an abstract should be between 150 and 250 words. Abstract should contain all the relevant information about your research. For scientific articles, the abstract should include: an introduction, methodology, research findings, conclusions which can be drawn from these results, and why this research matters. For non-scientific articles, abstract should proviode an overview of the wider academic field, the gap in the feild which the study fills (or error the study address), and the relevance of the research for the academic community. Any abbreviation defined in the abstract needs to be redefined upon first mention in the text. Please refrain from citing references in the abstract, whenever possible; however, if cited, put in the full reference details.

Keywords: 3 - 6 keywords
